Welcome to All Flags

this is a simple app that will add an overlay of a French, Lebanese, and Syrian flag onto your facebook profile photo

  • Step 1: Click on your profile photo on your Facebook page
  • Step 2: Click Options and click Download
  • Step 3: Back on this page, click "choose file" and navigate to where you downloaded your facebook profile
  • Step 4: Right click on the new photo and click "save as"
  • Step 4: Upload the new photo as your Facebook profile pic

Made by Quest Henkart, this ultra simple mini app adds French, Lebanese and Syrian flags on facebook profile photo. It is open source: Check out the Repo here, add improvements or make your own version
